Remove Office 2016 Add-ins to drastically improve startup times, applies to:

- Word 2016
- Excel 2016
- Powerpoint 2016

## Instructions

1. Open your Office 2016 product.

2. Select the `File` option from the Microsoft Office Ribbon menu.

   ![Microsoft Office 2016 Ribbon](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-remove-add-ins-from-office-2016/office-2016-ribbon.png))

3. Select `Options` in the left navigation bar

   ![Microsoft Office 2016 Options](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-remove-add-ins-from-office-2016/office-2016-options.png))

4. On the Add-ins window press `Go`

   ![Microsoft Office 2016 Add-ins](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-remove-add-ins-from-office-2016/office-2016-add-ins.png))

5. Deselect all Add-ins you don't need

   ![Microsoft Office 2016 Add-in Selector](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-remove-add-ins-from-office-2016/office-2016-add-in-selector.png))

6. Press `OK`

7. Now restart your Office 2016 product and enjoy lightning fast startups
