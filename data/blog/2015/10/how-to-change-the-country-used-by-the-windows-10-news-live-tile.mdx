Follow these steps to make the Windows 10 News App display news for your
specific country.

1. Open the Windows 10 Search Menu, type `news` and select the News application

   ![Windows 10 Search Menu](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-change-the-country-used-by-the-windows-10-news-live-tile/windows-10-search-menu.png))

2. Open the Setting windows by pressing the cog icon (bottom left)

   ![Windows 10 News App Main Window - English](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-change-the-country-used-by-the-windows-10-news-live-tile/windows-10-news-app-main-window-en.png))

3. On the settings window select your preferred country and choose `Close App`

   ![Windows 10 News App Settings Window](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-change-the-country-used-by-the-windows-10-news-live-tile/windows-10-news-app-settings-window.png))

4. Restart the news app, it should now show news for your specific country

   ![Windows 10 News App Main Window - Dutch](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-change-the-country-used-by-the-windows-10-news-live-tile/windows-10-news-app-main-window-nl.png))

5. Open the Start Menu to verify the News Live Tile has been updated as well

   ![Windows 10 Start Menu News Live Tile](/static/images/blog/2015/10/how-to-change-the-country-used-by-the-windows-10-news-live-tile/windows-10-start-menu-news-live-tile.png))
